Our Service Information

We are using two paths to deliver your cargo to Pakistan

AIR TRANSPORT

Transport by airplane makes the world a smaller place. For goods that need to be transported fast and over long distances, transport by airplane is perfect. Cargo Company arranges the transport of your shipments to all continents fast and safely via the leading European airports. The transports are executed only by the best airline companies. We take care of the entire transport, with the right knowledge of customs formalities and, if you need, from door to door. Flying is fast, but customs formalities can cause unnecessary delays. In a short time the air freight shipment needs to be prepared according to strict regulations. From correct packaging on air freight pallets to the proper customs documentations to arrange customs clearance; we can take care of everything for you. Furthermore, we have worldwide knowledge of specific import regulations and can consult you accordingly. With our experience and expertise Cargo Company ensures a smooth transport from start to delivery.

DEEP SEA SHIPPING

DTransport by sea is the cheapest and most environmentally-friendly way to transport goods over long distances. Deep sea shipping bridges the distance between continents. Cargo Company has many years of experience organising the transport of container shipments and solves logistics problems all over the world with the support of an extensive transport network. The deep sea container shipments run via the leading European container ports of Rotterdam, Antwerp, Hamburg and Bremen. Via these sea ports Cargo Company organises the import, export and cross trade of goods per container. We service both full container load (FCL) and less than container load (LCL). In case of LCL Cargo Company can organise shipments including groupage and consolidation of part loads. For your precious cargo we have a track and trace solution; this way the entire transport can be monitored from beginning to end, 24 hours a day. Furthermore, we can take care of gas measurements and, if necessary, the controlled degassing of containers. Cargo Company is also happy to take the customs formalities off your hands. We like to unburden our clients wherever we can.
